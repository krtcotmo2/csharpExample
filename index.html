Select all or specific salespeople. Select a date range. Permission levels either allow the user to only see their data or the data of their direct reports.

System taps into ConnectWise to get sales figures for closed deals and invoices paid during those dates.

Displays summary for chosen sales reps. The commissions are based on the type of sale made.

The data cn be exported out as an Excel file. The Excel format allows users to run calculations and possible dispute any discrepancies in the data from ConnectWise.

Expanding out the individual salesperson shows a breakdown of all sales and the invoices by paid and unpaid, grouped by the company.

Expanding the company details shows each individual item, the margins for the item and the amount of commission gained from that sale.
